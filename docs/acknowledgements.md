### New TeraSender upload module

* René Klomp and Edwin Schaap for their work on [the FileSender
  TeraByte Challenge](https://rp.os3.nl/2012-2013/p08/presentation.pdf) as part of their master in system and network
  engineering at Amsterdam University (UvA). This work resulted in a
  [research report](https://rp.os3.nl/2012-2013/p08/report.pdf) documenting how to increase the upload speed of
  FileSender and in the new TeraSender upload module in version 1.6.
  See the [blog articles](https://filesender.wordpress.com/category/terabyte-challenge/) for details.
